Use Money to Buy Time (twitter.com)

Published:   September 1, 2021

Tags:
Bookmark for: https://twitter.com/paraschopra/status/1432615157742510084?s=21

If you have enough to get basic life go by(for needs not for greed), extra money should be used to buy time. Initially you need t sell your time to make money, once there, basic needs taken care of start using money to buy time.

Tweet was inspired was

Take every opportunity you get to use money to buy time, and then proceed to “waste” that extra time in doing things that cannot be bought for money.

There are few things money can’t buy for me like health, family, friendship, trust etc.

Simply enjoy life to fullest to whatever be your defination. Money if very important part of life, but shouldn’t become life. Life is much more than that.
