So idea here is to reduce the distraction. One thought that was difficult for me to overcame was

I should have self-control, no such controls are needed.

Have less distraction and making bad habits(imo) difficult is powerful way to stay more focussed in case I drift.

  1. Videos on Home page.
  2. Suggested videos.
  3. Comments (Wasted time reading it, and endup think, why in this world did I read them).
