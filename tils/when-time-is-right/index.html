When Time Is Right

Tags:

Sometimes I get very important things from very weird place. I have this habit of wandering at odd place, without reason. One such place in digital world is youtube, my time-killer

While randomly watching youtube video, this You need to focus on giving your 100%, it doesn’t matter you reach on top or not, but you should give your 100 advice came in a video. It such a simple advice and probably would have listened to it 1000 times, but this time it actually made me think. Either these 30s are really good to me, I’m learning a lot in them or time is right something or other.

It had never been easy thing to imbibe, have never given 100% anywhere afaik, but want to give my 100% on things now and that is what I care about now. Probably it also joins with sholka, have listened thousand time before.

कर्मणये वाधिकारस्ते मां फलेषु कदाचन । मां कर्मफलहेतुर्भू: मांते संङगोस्त्वकर्मणि ।।

Sri Krishna said to Arjuna: You have a right to perform your prescribed duty, but you are not entitled to the fruits of action. Never consider yourself the cause of the results of your activities, and never be attached to not doing your duty."

Probably when time is right or you’re ready, everything falls in place.
