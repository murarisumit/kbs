Leaving current org, risk and rewards

Tags:

Background:

There have few things and also got new oppourtunities hence been planning to leave the existing org, meanwhile was having discussion my mentor(will ask him if I can put his name here) about my change and got really nice quote from him While changing of jobs you need to think in terms of risk and reward, and also one side quote the devil you know is better than one you don't. (dont' mean literal devil here but hope you understand meaning behind quote)

Risk:

If you join new org, you leaving something you know about(devil you know), how it functions and how to get things done there here, when you move to new org ensure you don’t know how it works, it’s new cognitive load. Hence the risk.

Reward:

One reward that you directly out the face is compensation. It gets balanced with industry standard, not sure why it so across industries. Same person in org is not compensated like new employee coming in. It’s more like you don’t treat your current customer well and spend lot of money is marketing to aquire new customers. There might be some reason probably a bit more google someday #todo

The other important one is: sometimes if you’re really disgusted by the place you work in, even new work to place gives you new fresh experience and meeting and working new peers would be great learning experience.
