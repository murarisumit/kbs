How to Handle Difficult Conversations

Published:   August 31, 2021

Tags:

I.AM.CALM model for handling difficult convesation, learnt it in grab, and found it helpful.

How do you handle difficult conversations with empathy and kindness? :thinking_face: Try the I.AM.CALM model below:

I = Intent should be positive

  • Phrase your intentions by sharing AND

A = Assumptions

  • Identify your bias and ask questions to know the full story

M = Make it safe

  • Encourage a safe environment by apologizing for your mistakes (if any) and establishing a mutual purpose.

C = Clarify the facts

  • Separate fact from fiction

A = Ask for other’s viewpoint

  • Ask open ended questions

L = Listening

  • Paraphrase what you think the other person meant

M = Manage Resolution & Action

  • Explore win-win solutions and agree on next steps
