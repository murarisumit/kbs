Not a fan of twitter, but using twitter to reach out to people get feedback, improve ourselves, make myself better and this note is to remind myself why enabled this feature of social sharing in this site.

This also gives me flexibility to adding supporting for any other platform that come in future, but still having website as a central source of truth of what I do.

Thanks to Sharingbuttons developer for creating them.
