What Is Not Love (youtube.com)

This youtube video(click the title, that is how bookmarks are arranged for all posts), is of Abraham Joshua Heshel Twerski, an Israeli-American Hasidic rabbi, a scion of the Chernobyl Hasidic dynasty, and a psychiatrist specializing in substance abuse.

External-love is what I’m going to give, not what I’m going to get. (This line is bit vague)

I good line that i need to keep in check is:

“People make serious mistake in thinking, that you give to those who you love, and the answer is you love to those whom you give” His point is, if I give something to you, I have invested myself in you, since self-love is given everybody loves themselves, now that part of me has become in you, there is part of me in you that I love.
