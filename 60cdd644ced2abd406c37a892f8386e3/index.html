Nithin Kamath of Zerodha Says True Financial Freedom Is About Acquiring Skills First Than Money

Modified:   May 15, 2022
Published:   August 13, 2021

Tags:
Ref: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ocvyLH6ccjc

This guy is simple to understand, no out of the world quotations. Can relate to him, seems like a neighbourhood elder brother(bhaiya) who you can talk to.

What he mentioned was, spend on skills. More like one message in a 3-idiot movie. Run after excellence, success will come to you. He isn’t sadhu and adds on to say to buy material assets like cars or houses. If a house gives peace of mind, improves quality of life, go buy a house. If cars make your life easy, use it.

Another important thing he mentioned is, we have to make money work for us to be financially independent and financial freedom is about being content. There are endless materialistic traps.

Just to few more bits to the post. There are three important assets

  • Time
  • Money
  • Skills

We need to optimise on these three bits.
